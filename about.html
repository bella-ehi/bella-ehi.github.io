A "Renaissance woman" refers to a multi-talented, curious, and accomplished woman, someone who excels in a wide range of fields and isn't afraid to explore both the arts and sciences, intellect and creativity, leadership and compassion. Inspired by the Renaissance Era (14th–17th centuries), when great thinkers like Leonardo da Vinci were celebrated for their broad range of talents. A Renaissance woman is well-rounded, intellectually driven, creative, and often an agent of cultural, social, or personal transformation. She embraces both STEM and the arts, entrepreneurship and activism, logic and imagination.

Our Story

HerRenaissance was born from a vision: to uplift and empower African women and girls who’ve been sidelined by society. Founded by Isabella E., our mission began with local outreach and now spans education, technology, creative expression, and business training.

Who can become part of our community?

  • Teen and young adult girls seeking empowerment, mentorship, or career pathways in tech, art, or entrepreneurship
  • Donors and sponsors (both local and international) who care about gender equity, education, and economic development
  • Volunteers and mentors — especially women in tech, business, and art who can share knowledge and experiences
  • NGOs, government programs, and community leaders who can support or partner in scaling impact
  • Educators and parents who influence the paths girls take and can help shift mindsets
